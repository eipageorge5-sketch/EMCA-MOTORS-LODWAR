Frequently Asked Questions

What spare parts do you stock?
We specialize in oil filters, air filters, brake pads, and other essential maintenance parts for common vehicles in Turkana.
Why is wheel alignment important?
It ensures even tyre wear, better fuel efficiency, and prevents pulling. We recommend checking every 10,000 km.
What's the difference between alignment and balancing?
Alignment adjusts wheel angles. Balancing corrects weight distribution to stop vibrations.
Do I need an appointment?
Walk-ins are welcome for parts, but we strongly recommend booking for alignment, balancing, or major repairs.
